Overview[]

The Stone Hatchet is the second available tool in ARK: Survival Evolved used for harvesting resources. Unlike its counterpart, the Stone Pickaxe, a Stone Hatchet requires the player to invest Engram Points to learn how to craft it.

The Stone Hatchet provides more wood than thatch when harvesting a tree, more hide than raw meat when harvesting a corpse, and more stone than flint when mining rocks.

Like the Stone Pickaxe, a Stone Hatchet can be used as a rudimentary weapon. It can be found in white and green beacons.

The Stone Hatchet is superseded by the Metal Hatchet.
